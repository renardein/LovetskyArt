Effaced Itinerary

Film information

  • Original Title: Стертый Маршрут
  • Released: December 1, 2022
  • Actors: Nikita Lovetsky, Valeriia Radchenko
  • Music: Neachtar, rainfuture
  • Artwork: Nikita Lovetsky, Misty Night
  • Festivals: None
  • Additional: Drama, experimental

Synopsis

A young artist travels by train from his small town to art school in New York City where he is to continue his studies. Along the way, he surrealistically falls into a time loop in which his painful imagination of working on a painting and endless road are combined.
